Paradigmshift

The video of the key-note presentation of Pieter Haasnoot on 'Paradigm Shift' will be uploaded here soon.

(WdKA symposium 'Redesigning Business', Maassilo Rotterdam, November 20 2014)

Peter Haasnoot's mission is to welcome exponential development technologies as sources for positive change and societal value. As he sees it, recent developments towards radical transparency, empowerment of individuals, traceability and a collaborative network economy are reshaping society and leading to business innovation; one of his statements is 'small beats big'. Haasnoot will talk about the concept of paradigm shift and its consequences for businesses.

Pieter Haasnoot sees himself as a humanist innovator. His company Up Next is a social enterprise that uses the power of business to bring about positive change. Up Next consists of a network of people, who collectively work at the edges of their disciplines in order to discover new ways of solving societal problems. They seek to be agents of positive change, transforming society by connecting societal value, business and technology.
